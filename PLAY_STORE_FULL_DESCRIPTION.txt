🌍 WELCOME TO WEATHER APP

Your trusted companion for accurate, real-time weather forecasts anywhere in the world. Whether you're planning your day, traveling, or just curious about the weather, Weather App delivers precise forecasts with a beautiful, intuitive interface.

⭐ FEATURES

✓ Real-Time Weather Updates
Get instant access to current weather conditions including temperature, humidity, wind speed, and "feels like" temperature. Our data is updated continuously to ensure accuracy.

✓ Hourly Forecasts
Plan your day hour by hour with detailed 12-hour forecasts. See temperature trends and weather changes throughout the day to prepare accordingly.

✓ 7-Day Forecast
Look ahead with our comprehensive 7-day forecast. Plan weekends, trips, and outdoor activities with confidence knowing what the weather will bring.

✓ Global Location Support
Search weather for any city worldwide. Select your country, state/province, and city to get localized weather information instantly. No GPS required - you control your location.

✓ Beautiful UI Design
Experience weather information through an elegant, gradient-based interface with smooth animations and intuitive navigation.

✓ Quick Location Selection
Easily switch between locations. Pre-filled options make it quick to check weather for different cities and regions.
